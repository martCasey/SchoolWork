Projects

During my time working as a student at ITT, I need to work on a handful of projects from time to time as per the requirements of my assignments. Some of these projects were individual, and others I needed to work on with other people in a group. The projects and there details are shown here:

Work Experience Project:

I spent the second half of my 3rd Year of my computing course engaged with the Work Experience module in my course. I performed my work experience at the Department of Social Protection. During my time working at the Department I worked with two other interns from DIT, to develop a Photocopier Information Management System for internal use by staff members in the Department.

Second Year Project 1

For my second year of computing in my course, I need to work on an software based project as per the requirement of my Software Development module. This project required myself and two other students to work together and build a ticket booking console based application for a cinema, using java and sql.

Second Year Project 2

The next project that I had to do as per the requirement of another module in my second year of computing was that of an Lesuire Centre Website. I also need to work on this project with two other students, though they were completely different students than who I worked with on my last project.

First Year Project 2

The first big project I need to perform as per the requirement of one of my computing modules was not necessiarly a computing focused project, as all computing CA's at that point were in lab tests. No, instead this project was a Social Media Research Project that I and two other students need to conduct. For the project we need to interview a business owner and devise a possible social media strategy they coudl use.
